SMART™ MBA Consulting Capstone

Westcliff University’s SMART™ MBA Consulting capstone
program enables students to be management consultants for
global powerhouses, regional brands, non-profits, and startups.
As a result, students refine communication skills and develop
creative thinking abilities at a pace and intensity that exceeds
other programs.

How Does It Work?

  • Work with small student teams of no more than 5 people with support and guidance from faculty, executives, and mentors
  • Research, analyze, and develop recommendations for executives
  • Opportunity to provide results and resolutions to C-Suite executives
